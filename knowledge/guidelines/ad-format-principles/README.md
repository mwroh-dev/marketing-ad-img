# Global Ad Format Principles

Brand-agnostic principles. No brand, product, or category specifics belong here.

---

## Meta Ad Formats

| format_id               | ratio  | canvas (px)  | primary placement       |
|-------------------------|--------|--------------|-------------------------|
| meta_square_1_1         | 1:1    | 1080 × 1080  | Feed, Marketplace       |
| meta_feed_4_5           | 4:5    | 1080 × 1350  | Feed (max vertical)     |
| meta_story_9_16         | 9:16   | 1080 × 1920  | Stories, Reels          |
| meta_landscape_1_91_1   | 1.91:1 | 1200 × 628   | Link ads, Right column  |

---

## Safe Zones

Text and key brand elements must stay within safe zones to avoid UI chrome overlap.

- **Stories / Reels (9:16):** Keep critical content between 14% from top and 20% from bottom.
  Top and bottom strips are covered by profile UI and CTA buttons.
- **Feed (1:1, 4:5):** No platform chrome overlay, but thumbnail crops apply when shared.
  Keep focal point centered or in the upper 60% of the canvas.
- **Landscape (1.91:1):** Link preview crops to ~1:1 in some placements; center the focal point.

---

## Text Overlay Guidance

Meta recommends keeping text overlay under 20% of total image area for delivery quality.
Text-heavy images may receive reduced reach without explicit policy violation.
For copy-heavy concepts, use the caption/body field rather than baking text into the image.

---

## Format Selection Heuristic

- Cold audience, awareness goal → 1:1 or 4:5 (feed dominance).
- Retargeting, high intent → 9:16 (immersive, captures full attention).
- Driving traffic to a link → 1.91:1 (optimized for link preview rendering).
